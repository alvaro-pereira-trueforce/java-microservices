FAQ

Authentication Token

  • What is the Authentication token?
    The Authentication token is a code we use to setup the communication between Facebook/Instagram and Zendesk.
  • Does the Authentication token expire?
    Yes, it expires 60 days after you integrated Instagram and Zendesk for the first time.
  • What do I need to do if the Authentication token has expired?
    You need to update your Instagram Channel configuration on Zendesk, this action will renew your Authentication token automatically, you can do it at anytime.

    Note: After your Authentication token is renewed it becomes a token with no expiration.

Instagram Channel and Zendesk integration

  • My Instagram Channel integration stopped creating new tickets, what happened?
    If you did not renewed your Authentication token before and you integrated Instagram Channel with Zendesk around 60 days ago, you will need to update your Instagram Channel configuration, this will make your Authentication token to be renewed automatically.

    If you already renewed your Authentication token and still having issues, please take a look to this points:

    • Review your Facebook configuration, the apps section.
    • Ensure the Instagram Account you are using is an Instagram Business Account.
    • Ensure the Instagram BA is linked to the Facebook Page you chose on the integration.
