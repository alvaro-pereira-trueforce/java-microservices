Getting Started

To start talking with your customer using your Instagram Business Account, install our “Instagram Channel” app from Zendesk Marketplace. Then add your Instagram Business Account.

Instagram Channel - Requirements before configuring Zendesk:

Before start integrating Zendesk Support and Instagram you will need the next point.

  • An active Instagram Business Account linked to an active Facebook Page. To know how to change to an Instagram Business Account and link it to a Facebook Page follow these steps.

(Note: Please follow these steps using the Instagram mobile app especially when linking it to a Facebook page)

  1. From your existing profile in the mobile app, tap on Settings. (If you don’t have a profile yet, install Instagram on your device and follow the prompts to create a personal profile).
  2. Choose the Switch to Business Account option.
  3. On the Set Up Your Business Account page, review your business’s contact information, make any changes and tap Continue.
  4. On the Connect to Facebook page, choose the Facebook page you will link to your Instagram Business Account and tap Done.

Zendesk Support Steps:

Preparation - Adding an Instagram Business Account to Zendesk:

  1. Install the Instagram Channel Integration app from the Zendesk Marketplace.
  2. Go to your Zendesk admin page and look out for Channel Integrations button at the side bar.
  3. Select our “Instagram Channel” app.
  4. Review the ‘General Information’ tab and make changes if applicable.
  5. Select the “Accounts” tab.
  6. In the pop-up page, set the Instagram integration name for your company.
  7. Click on ‘Connect to Facebook’ button.
  8. Login to the Facebook account that has the Facebook page you will use if applicable.
  9. In the next pop-up page, select the Facebook page which has a linked Instagram Business account.
  10. Press the “Submit” button.
  11. That’s it! Start receiving your Instagram posts.

The new account is ready to start receiving your Instagram post as soon the customer start to comment.
