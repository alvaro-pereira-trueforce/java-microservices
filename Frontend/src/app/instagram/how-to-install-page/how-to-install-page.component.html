Getting Started

The Instagram Channel Integration needs:

  • An active Instagram Business account. To know how to change to an Instagram Business account click here.
  • An active Facebook page which has linked an Instagram Business account. To know how to link an Instagram Business account to a Facebook page click here.

Zendesk Support Steps:

  1. Go to your Zendesk admin page and look out for Channel Integrations button at the side bar.
  2. Select our “Instagram Channel” app.
  3. Review the ‘General Information’ tab and make changes if applicable.
  4. Select the “Accounts” tab.
  5. In the pop-up page, set the Instagram integration name for your company.
  6. Click on ‘Connect to Facebook’ button.
  7. Login to the Facebook account that has the Facebook page you will use if applicable.
  8. In the next pop-up page, select the Facebook page which has a linked Instagram Business account.
  9. Press the “Submit” button.

The new account is ready to start receiving your Instagram post as soon the customer start to comment.
